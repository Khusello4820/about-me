About Me

I am Khuselo Sofohlo, a dedicated and ambitious Computer Science student at Wits University with a passion for problem-solving, software development, and artificial intelligence. Originally from Kanana in Lusikisiki, I have always been driven by curiosity and a strong work ethic, which has led me to excel academically, earning six distinctions in my first year. Beyond academics, I am actively involved in leadership and mentorship as a First Year Experience Mentor and the Maintenance & Welfare Officer of my residence's House Committee. I also balance my time with fitness, skating, and volunteering as an usher at my church. With strong analytical and leadership skills, I aim to make an impact in the tech industry while continuously growing and learning.
